You’re not alone — and you’re not broken.

This space was created with one goal: to support Miami University students navigating the challenges of gambling and social media addiction. College life can be exciting, but it also comes with pressures, distractions, and stress that can make unhealthy habits easy to fall into and hard to break.

We understand how overwhelming it can feel to be caught in patterns that affect your focus, your mental health, your relationships, and your future. Whether you’re spending hours chasing wins on betting apps or scrolling endlessly on social media, this site is here to help you take back control.

What We Offer:

  • Resources to help you understand addiction and how it works — without judgment.
  • Tools and tips for building healthier habits and breaking cycles.
  • Real stories from students who’ve been there and made it through.
  • Connections to campus counseling, support groups, and off-campus help.

We’re here to remind you: seeking help isn’t a sign of weakness. It’s a sign of strength, self-respect, and courage.

Take your first step toward change. We’re with you every step of the way.
