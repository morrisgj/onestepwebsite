Find Help

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling addiction, it's important to seek help. Here are some resources available:

National Resources

  • National Problem Gambling Helpline: Call 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537) for confidential, 24/7 support. This helpline connects individuals to local resources and offers support for all 50 states and U.S. territories. More information is available at https://www.ncpgambling.org/helpline/.
  • Gamblers Anonymous: A fellowship of people who share their experiences, strength, and hope with each other to solve their common problem and help others recover from a gambling problem. Find local meetings at https://www.gamblersanonymous.org/ga/.

Local Resources in Miami

  • Miami University Counseling Center: Offers confidential counseling services to students dealing with gambling addiction. For more information, visit https://www.miamioh.edu/student-life/counseling-center/.
  • Gamblers Anonymous Miami Meetings: Local meetings are available for those seeking support in the Miami area. Find more details at https://www.gamblersanonymous.org/ga/meetings.
